About Me

Hi, I'm Samuel Chinedu Udoba, born on June 16th, 2003, and currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Smart Automation at FH Campus 02, Graz. My journey has been anything but ordinary — and I'm proud of how far it's brought me.

I began my academic life at Spring Nursery and Primary School (2005–2013), then moved on to All Hallows Seminary, Onitsha where I studied from 2013 to 2019. After that, I continued at Bigard Memorial Seminary, Enugu from 2021 to 2023. These years shaped not just my education but also my discipline, values, and drive to keep growing.

In 2023, I made a bold and life-changing decision — to pivot into technology and pursue my deep interest in automation, systems, and intelligent machines. That led me here, to Austria, where I’m fully immersed in the world of Smart Automation — and I’ve never looked back.

What makes my path unique is that I taught myself programming from scratch. With no formal background, I dived into tutorials, projects, and hands-on learning, gradually mastering Python, CSS, JavaScript, and HTML. For me, programming isn’t just about writing code — it’s about solving real problems, automating ideas, and creating things that work.

Outside the screen, I’m a huge fan of football and basketball — sports that keep me active, focused, and balanced. They’ve taught me discipline, adaptability, and the importance of consistency — lessons I apply daily in my tech journey.

I’m constantly learning, building, and evolving — and I hope this portfolio reflects not just my technical abilities but the passion, persistence, and personality behind every project I take on.
